Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does VoteWise AI help voters?

VoteWise AI provides instant answers to election-related questions, helps find polling booths, and guides first-time voters through the entire process with multilingual support.

Can first-time voters use this platform?

Absolutely! VoteWise AI is specifically designed for first-time voters with step-by-step guidance and easy-to-understand explanations in multiple languages.

Does it support Hindi and other languages?

Yes! We support multiple languages including Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, and more through Google Translate integration.

Can I set election reminders?

Yes! You can set reminders for registration deadlines and election day, which sync directly to your Google Calendar for timely notifications.

Is this an official election site?

No, VoteWise AI is an independent civic education platform and is NOT affiliated with any government election commission.
