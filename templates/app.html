Election Process Guide

1. Voter Registration

Register yourself as a voter on the Election Commission website or at your local Electoral Registration Office. You'll need valid ID proof and address proof.

2. Verify Your Name

Check that your name appears in the electoral roll for your constituency. You can verify this online through the Election Commission portal.

3. Know Your Polling Booth

Find your assigned polling station using the voter slip or our Polling Booth Finder tool. Locate it on Google Maps for easy navigation.

4. Gather Required Documents

Carry your Voter ID (EPIC) or valid photo ID proof to the polling station. Accepted IDs include Aadhaar, Passport, or Driving License.

5. Cast Your Vote

Go to your polling station on election day, verify your identity, and cast your vote. Remember to follow the voting guidelines inside the booth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to vote?

Any Indian citizen aged 18 years or above on the qualifying date is eligible to vote.

How do I check if I'm registered?

Visit the Election Commission website and search with your name and constituency.

What if my name is not in the list?

You can register as a new voter through the online or offline process at your local electoral office.

Can I vote from anywhere?

No, you must vote at your assigned polling station in your constituency.
